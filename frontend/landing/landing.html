FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is GIG SUI?
GIG SUI is a decentralized platform built on the Sui blockchain that connects freelancers with clients worldwide. Our platform enables secure, transparent, and efficient gig economy transactions without traditional intermediaries.
How does payment work on GIG SUI?
Payments on GIG SUI are handled through smart contracts on the Sui blockchain. When a gig is completed, the payment is automatically released from escrow to the freelancer. We support various cryptocurrencies, and all transactions are secured by blockchain technology.
What types of jobs can I find or post on GIG SUI?
GIG SUI supports a wide range of digital services including but not limited to: software development, graphic design, content writing, digital marketing, blockchain development, and consulting services. Any digital service that can be delivered remotely can be offered on our platform.
How do you ensure quality and trust?
We maintain quality through our reputation system built on blockchain technology. Every completed job contributes to a freelancer's reputation score, which is immutable and transparent. Additionally, our escrow system protects both parties during transactions.
What are the fees for using GIG SUI?
GIG SUI charges a minimal platform fee compared to traditional freelance platforms. Our fee structure is transparent and primarily covers the cost of processing blockchain transactions. Exact fees depend on the service type and transaction size.
